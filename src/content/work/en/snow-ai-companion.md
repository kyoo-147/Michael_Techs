## Context

Snow AI Companion is a child-safe AI companion designed for autistic children, focusing on gentle interaction, emotional support, daily routines, and parental control. The product combines a kid-friendly interface, voice-based AI conversation, animated character feedback, routine guidance, and a safety layer that limits open-ended AI behavior.
